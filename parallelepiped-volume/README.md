# Moment of Inertia

In this mini-project using the Monte Carlo method, calculate the moment of inertia of a parallelepiped with the following side lengths (in arbitrary units):

* $a=4$
* $b=2$
* $c=3$

  We assume the parallelepiped has a constant density of $\rho_0 = 3$ (in arbitrary units). In this mini-project we calculate the moment of inertia with respect to an axis passing through one of its edges, and we compare our result with the known analytical value. Next, we assume the parallelepiped has a linearly varying density along a chosen direction. Final we calculate the moments of inertia with respect to the axes both orthogonal and parallel to the direction of the density variation.
